Declaration

By sending this application you agree:

  • the information in this application is complete and correct as far as you know
  • to report any changes to your circumstances promptly
  • that you'll use the loan for one of the qualifying reasons
See the qualifying reasons for a loan

You can use a Budgeting Loan to pay for:

  • furniture or household items
  • clothing or footwear
  • rent in advance
  • costs linked to moving house
  • maintenance, security or improvements to your home
  • travelling costs within the UK
  • costs linked to getting a new job
  • maternity costs
  • funeral costs
  • repaying hire purchase loans
  • repaying loans taken for the above items

Your benefit may be stopped if you deliberately give wrong information that would affect your claim, or don’t report changes promptly.
