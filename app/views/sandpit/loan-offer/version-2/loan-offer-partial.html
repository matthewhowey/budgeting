How your loan has been calculated

The loan amount is based on:

  • the amount you owe the Social Fund
  • your regular debt repayments
  • how much you can afford to pay each week

If you accept:

  • you'll repay £6.08 a week, which will be automatically taken from your Jobseeker's Allowance
  • your new Jobseeker's Allowance payment will be £50 a week for 52 weeks
  • you'll still have to repay the loan if you stop claiming benefits

If you're not happy with the loan offer, you can ask for the decision to be reviewed. Write to JobCentre Plus in the next 28 days explaining why you think the decision is wrong.
