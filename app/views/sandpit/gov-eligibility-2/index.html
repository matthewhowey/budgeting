Eligibility start page

Insight

People selectively read pages by focusing on common key words or phrases and retain/recall information based on their focus and people pick up additional information if they are given a chance to read the content again.

People do not understand benefit entitlement sufficiently to make a correct decision about eligibility

If:
We separate the info about benefits and sums of money on the ‘Check your eligibility’ start page
Then:
Users will have a better understanding about which benefits they need to be claiming
Because:
Users focus on the ‘£’s
If:
Eligibility trigger words & phrases are used when explaining eligibility
Then:
Users will retain information they don’t currently understand
Because:
Eye tracking research revealed keywords that users focus on
