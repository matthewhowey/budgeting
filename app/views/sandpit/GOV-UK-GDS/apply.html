4. How to apply

You can apply for a Budgeting Loan online

Before you start, you’ll need to have details about your regular debt repayments, eg hire purchases or loans. You won’t need details of your normal living expenses like gas and electric charges or food bills.

You can’t make an application if any of the following apply to you:

  • you owe more than a total of £1,500 for a Crisis Loan or Budgeting Loan (or for both combined)
  • you or your partner have savings of more than £1,000 (£2,000 if either of you are over 61)
  • you’re involved in a trade dispute, eg a strike or walkout

Apply now

Questions about your claim

Contact Jobcentre Plus if you have any questions about your claim. Allow 15 working days after posting your form before contacting them - they may not have processed your claim before then.

If you’re unhappy with a decision

You can ask for a Budgeting Loan decision to be reviewed if you're not happy with it. To do this, you must write to Jobcentre Plus within 28 days of the decision, explaining why you think it was wrong.

If you still think the decision was wrong after the review, you can ask for a further review by the office of the Independent Case Examiner. This review should take no longer than 21 working days.

Other ways to apply

You can download and fill in form SF500. Your claim can take up to 15 working days. Include your mobile phone number to get updates on its progress.
