3. Check your eligibility

To apply for a Budgeting Loan:

  • you must need more than £100
  • you must have been getting Income Support, income-based Jobseeker's Allowance, income-related Employment and Support Allowance or Pension Credit for the past 6 months (and you must still be getting one of these benefits when you apply)
  • you must not owe the Social Fund more than £1,500 for a Crisis Loan or Budgeting Loan
  • you and your partner must not have combined savings of more than £1,000 (£2,000 if either of you are over 61)
  • you must not be involved in industrial action (eg a strike, walk-out or lock-out)

You can apply for a loan online if you meet the 5 eligiblity points above
