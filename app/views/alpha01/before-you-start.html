Before you start

To get a Budgeting Loan:

  • You must have been getting at least 1 of the following benefits for the past 6 months (Income Support, Income-based Jobseeker's Allowance, Employment and Support Allowance or Pension Credit).
  • You must not be involved in a trade dispute (eg industrial action, a strike, walk out, lock-out or any other dispute about work).
  • You and your partner must not have savings over £1000. If either you or your partner is 62 or over you must not have savings over £2000.
  • You must not be getting Universal Credit.
