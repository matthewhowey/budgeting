Declaration

By sending this application you agree:

  • the information in this application is complete and correct as far as you know
  • to report any changes to your circumstances promptly
  • that you will use the loan for one of the qualifying reasons
Qualifying reasons for a loan
  • Furniture or household equipment
  • Clothing or footware
  • Rent in advance or house moving expense
  • Maintenance, security or improvements to the home
  • Travelling expenses within the UK
  • Expenses associated with getting a new job
  • Maternity costs
  • Funeral costs
  • Repaying hire purchase
  • Repaying loans taken for the above items

Your benefit may be stopped if you deliberately give wrong information that would affect your claim, or don't report changes promptly.
