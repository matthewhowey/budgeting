Before you start

To get a Budgeting Loan:

  • you must not owe the Social Fund more than £1,500 for a Crisis Loan or Budgeting Loan
  • you and your partner must not have combined savings of more than £1,000 (£2,000 if either of you are over 61)
  • you must not be involved in a trade dispute (eg a strike, walk-out or lock-out)
